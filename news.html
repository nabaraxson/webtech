Amity University Strategic Partnership with Thunderbird to Bring Global Leadership Program for Future Leaders


3rd July 2018

Amity University has recently partnered with Thunderbird to bring a wider access to global leadership opportunities amongst working professionals. India's No. 1 not-for-profit Pvt. University has now come up with dynamic “Global Leadership Program” under this tie-up that brings together the University and the Institution to offer future leaders a broad range of higher education and skill enhancement in global leadership.

 

The in-demand one-month certificate program is a blended course, consists of four modules that help executives to understand the global leadership success strategies. The strategic curriculum is categorized into online modules and in-person sessions that help to understand the fundamentals of leadership and lead across various cultures through cross-cultural intelligence. Hence, the curriculum uses an interdisciplinary approach that addresses both the opportunities and challenges of conducting business in today’s evolving environment. So, now the working professionals can learn to lead with purpose & impact with this program, and get the leadership edge to drive towards success.

 

This unbeatable international collaboration will give working professionals an opportunity to study at internationally-renowned and accredited university while gaining an in-depth exposure in a specific global business focus area. Thus, preparing them to adapt to and excel in a global work environment.

 

About Thunderbird

 

Thunderbird is the world’s No.1 ranked school of international business with nearly 70 years of experience developing leaders with the global mindset, business skills, and social responsibility necessary to create real, sustainable value for organizations, communities, and the world.
