Amity Online Awarded with Internationally Acclaimed EOCCS Certification for Online MBA and BBA Programs


04 Jun 2018

Amity University Online recently received an endorsement from EOCCS for Online MBA and BBA Programs on 31st May 2018 post review/assessment by International Review Panels. EOCCS is a highly prestigious and internationally acclaimed certification of EFMD (European Foundation for Management Development). Amity Online is delighted to be amongst the first and the only programs across all institutions in India to receive this globally recognized certification. The validity of this international certificate is for 3 years.

The Online Course Certification System (EOCCS) of EFMD is an international system, designed to evaluate the quality of online business and/or management programs, based on high quality international benchmarks in the use of digital technology in teaching and learning. It also evaluates the online courses in the context of rapidly evolving technologies within the framework of:

  • Institutional Context
  • Course Composition
  • Course Delivery and Operations
  • Quality Assurance Processes

 

This valuable and globally acclaimed award recognizes the excellence achieved by Amity University Online in all aspects of imparting Open, Distance and Online education to millions of students across the globe.

About EOCCS Certification (EFMD Global Network)

EOCCS is a newly launched certification system for online courses and eLearning designed for business schools and corporate development and built by a small team from the European EFMD group (known for having developed the internationally renowned EQUIS accreditation). So far, 39 courses of 14 renowned institutions worldwide have been certified by EOCCS, since its launch.
